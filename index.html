§02 / house rules

Six things we do not negotiate.

01

If we don't love it, we don't build it.

Every project on this site started with one of us going "wait, but what if…" out loud at an inconvenient time.

02

The brief is a problem we are personally stuck on.

No client work. No outside roadmap. We build the thing whose absence is annoying us today.

03

Beauty is the floor, not the ceiling.

If it ships ugly it isn't ready. Type, motion, micro-copy — all of it earns its keep.

04

Small bets, deep work, often.

We'd rather ship six small obsessions than one bloated quarterly plan. Easier to love. Easier to delete.

05

The roadmap is whatever we can't stop thinking about this week.

Yes that is a planning method. No we will not be writing it down.

06

If you can feel us in the product, we did it right.

The opposite of generic is fingerprints. We try to leave plenty.

Got something you can't stop thinking about?

We don't do client work, but we collect obsessions. If yours is loud enough, send it our way. Worst case you make a friend.
